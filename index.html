What is Contact Intelligence?

After 30 years with various major transnational corporations and industry bodies in Australia and U.K., employed in positions varying from In-House Legal Advisor to Company Secretary and Manager, Alan Riley realised an urgent need for specialised assistance within the areas of Tendering and Contract Administration, Dispute Prevention, Management and Resolution – ultimately to prevent and avoid the need for legal intervention.


Armed with an Honours degree in law, qualifications in Arbitration and Mediation and extensive practical experience in the corporate arena, Alan Riley entered the Consultancy Service market in 1995, to cater to the ever-increasing need for Contract Intelligence, primarily within the Construction and Engineering Industry.


In 2006, following a successful yet demanding period building an impressive database of clientele, Alan Riley was approached with a business proposal to incorporate additional services of Training Seminars, Quantity Surveying and Project Management Support in his portfolio. As a result Contract Intelligence Pty Ltd (CIPL) was formed whilst maintaining the core services provided by Alan Riley. However, CIPL ceased trading in September 2013.
